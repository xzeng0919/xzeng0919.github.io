Research Interest

I am a Ph.D. student working under the advisement of Prof. Kenta Nakai at the Institute of Medical Science, The University of Tokyo. My research focuses on spatial transcriptomics, gene regulatory network analysis and brain evolution, using Ciona intestinalis as a model organism. I have a broad interest in applying machine learning methods in next-generation sequencing data to elucidate the complex biological questions!
